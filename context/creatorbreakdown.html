Adlib Archive Catalogue – Context – Adlib Parent unit / Child unit

Requirement: Mandatory for parent/child records in a hierarchical record structure

Refer to local procedure:

• Adlib - Guidelines for entering Level in the Identity Statement tab and linking records in a hierarchy

For a single oral history, or a single interview recorded over a number of sessions, and the interview does not form part of a larger collection. Creator None Author/artist Interviewee is author/artist, listed first Author/artist The interviewer is author/artist, listed second For a collection of oral histories where all interviews are conducted by the same interviewer Creator Interviewer is Creator (at the highest level that applies) Author/artist Interviewee is author/artists (at appropriate level records) For a collection of oral histories of the same project and the project has an identified name Creator Project name is creator (at the highest level that applies) Author/artist Interviewees are author/artists listed first (at appropriate level records) Author/artist Interviewers are author/artists listed second (at appropriate level records) For a collection of oral histories all of the interviewee, not otherwise related and recorded by different interviewers and at different times, Creator Interviewee is creator (at the highest level that applies) Author/artist Interviewers are author/artists listed second (at appropriate level records)
