Adlib Archive Catalogue – Context – Creator

Requirement: As appropriate

For advice on identifying a creator and applying an access point, refer to:

• ISAD (G) 3.2.1 Name of creator

• ISAD (G) Section 2.4 – 'Do not repeat information at a lower level of description that has already been given at a higher level'

With reference to:

• DACS: 2.6 Name of creator(s)

For guidelines on creating authority records for creators, refer to:

• Local procedure 'Adlib - Creating name authorities'

• RDA Section 3: Recording Attributes of Agents

With reference to the minimum requirements for Archival Authority Records:

• DACS - Chapter 9 - Archival Authority Records

• Authorized form of name

• Type of entity

• Dates of existence

• Authority record identifier.

AND NOTE

• DACS prescribes dates of existence within an authority record as a minimum requirement, however, this does mean prescribe that dates should be included within the authorised form of name where there is no requirement for disambiguation.

• Authority records in Library Congress Name Authority files (LCNA), or within Libraries Australia Authorities (LAA) that are not disambiguated with dates, can be used in Adlib if a reference to dates of existence is made elsewhere within the Adlib authority record .

• Where an authority exists in LCNA or LAA and there is no reference to dates of existence, then update scope notes and/or non-preferred names, but retain the authorised form of name.
