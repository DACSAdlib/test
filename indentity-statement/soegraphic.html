Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement – Graphic Materials – Statement of Responsibility

Graphic materials

When recording Statements of Responsibility for Graphic materials refer to:

•DCRM (G)1G. Statements of responsibility

And conform to the:

•BIBCO Standard Record RDA Metadata Application Profile BSR RDA MAP

And follow the advice to:

•Generally transcribe all statements of responsibility relating to title proper found in the preferred source of information

Follow the advice at DCRM (G) 1G0.

•Do not supply or devise a statement of responsibility
