Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement – Date (Early) and Date (Late)

Requirement: Mandatory

Local Rules:

• Enter Date (early) and Date (late) dates in all records as appropriate.

• Date (early) – record the start or early date or begin date of the creationof the unit of description.

• Date (late) – record the end or late date of the creation of the unit ofdescription.

• Enter the date according to the ISO 8601 format: yyyy–mm–dd.

Examples

• March 4, 1878

• Date (early): 1878-03-04 Date (late): 1878–03–04

• 2005-2006

• Date (early): 2005-01-01 Date (late): 2006–12–31

• 1900

• Date (early): 1900-01-01 Date (late): 1900–12–31

• May 1965

• Date (early): 1965-05-01 Date (late): 1965–05–31

• Approximately 1808-1910

• Date (early): 1808-01-01 Date (late): 1910–12–31
