Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement – Title – Graphic Materials

Graphic materials

When describing Graphic materials follow the advice at:

• Descriptive Cataloging of Rare Materials (Graphic Materials) 2013 DCRM (G) Section 1. Title and Statement of Responsibility Area

And conform to the:

• BIBCO Standard Record RDA Metadata Application Profile (BSR RDA MAP)

Thus:

• Do not apply the instruction at DCRM (G) 1A1 to include a general material designation in the title

• When supplying or devising a title – Do not apply the instruction at DCRM (G) 1F–1F2 to enclose devised or supplied titles in square brackets

• When supplying or devising a title – Ensure that a note is left, detailing that the title has been supplied or devised.

• If required, include an alternative title (see Notes and description Control – Variant title field)

• In general, do not apply the instruction at DCRM (G) 1B7 to abridge long titles

• The instructions at DCRM (G) 1B can be used without further reference to the BSR RDA MAP

In addition:

• Do not apply the instruction at DCRM (G) 0E to areas with a period-space-dash-space (. -- )
