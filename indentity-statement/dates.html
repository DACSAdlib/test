Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement – Date

Requirement: Mandatory

Follow general guidelines in:

• ISAD (G): 3.1.3. Dates

• DACS: 2.4 Dates

When recording dates

• Apply the recommendation at DACS 2.4.12. Estimated date ranges, and do not abbreviate terms of approximation.

• Where the earliest or latest date of a range is estimated, apply 'approximately' at the start of the statement, and elaborate with an explanatory note.

For known exact single dates:

• DO NOT apply the advice at DACS 2.4.14 to order the elements of a date as year-month-day, and prefer instead the order day-month-year.

Graphic materials

For Graphic Materials, follow the guidelines at:

• DCRM (G) Section 4D

• See also, BSR-RDA-MAP concerning numbers expressed as words

AND refer to:

• BSR RDA MAP: (date of production, publication, manufacture)

AND, as directed in BSR RDA MAP, apply advice in:

• RDA 2.7.6, Date of production; also refer to RDA 1.9.2. for supplied or estimated dates

• When recording dates, do not abbreviate terms implying estimation.

Moving Images (Pilot project 2020)

For Moving Images, follow the guidelines at

• RDA: 2.7.– 2.10 Date of –publication – production – manufacture – distribution
