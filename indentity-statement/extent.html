Adlib Archive Catalogue – Identity statement – Extent

Requirement: Mandatory

Follow general guidelines in:

• ISAD (G) 3.1.5 Extent and medium of the unit

Where the above lacks specificity:

• DACS 2.5 Extent

Graphic Materials

For Graphic Materials, apply rules at:

• DCRM (G): Section 5

AND apply instructions within:

• BSR-RDA-MAP - Instruction and Elements - 'Extent'

•'Apply Descriptive Cataloging of Rare Materials (DCRM) conventions when recording extent; however, do not use abbreviations.'

Moving image materials (Pilot project 2020)

For moving image materials, refer to:
