Oral History Creator/ Author Artist breakdown

For a single oral history, or a single interview recorded over a number of sessions and the interview does not form part of a larger collection

• Creator: Interviewer

• Author/artist: Interviewee is author/artist

For a collection of oral histories where all interviews are conducted by the same interviewer

• Creator: Interviewer is Creator (at the highest level that applies)

• Author/artist: Interviewee is author/artists (at appropriate level records)

For a collection of oral histories of the same project and the project has an identified name

• Creator: Project name is creator (at the highest level that applies)

• Author/artist: Interviewers are author/artists listed second (at appropriate level records)

• Author/artist: Interviewees are author/artists listed first (at appropriate level records)

For a collection of oral histories all of the interviewee, not otherwise related and recorded by different interviewers and at different times

• Creator: Interviewee is creator (at the highest level that applies)

• Author/artist: Interviewers are author/artists listed second (at appropriate level records)
