Adlib Archive Catalogue – Notes and description Control – Archive Notes (Signatures and inscriptions)

Requirement: As appropriate

Record information about signatures, inscriptions, marks, monograms, or the like that appear on the unit being described and that are considered important. If important, indicate where such signatures and inscriptions appear. Do not record the actual signature if it is already included in the description

Follow general guidelines in:

• ISAD (G) 3.6.1. Notes

With reference to:

• DACS 7. Notes

When describing Graphic Materials refer also to:

• DCRM (G) 1A2 Sources of information (Required when statement of resposibility does not appear on or with the material)

• DCRM (G) 7B6.2. Signature, marks, monograms, etc.

• See also BSR RDA MAP Note on statement of responsibility

• RDA 2.17.3. Note on statement of responsibility

When describing Moving Image materials (Pilot project 2020) refer also to:

• RDA 2.17.3. Note on statement of responsibility
