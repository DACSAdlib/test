Adlib Archive Catalogue – Allied Materials – Existence and location of originals

Requirement: As appropriate

Used to indicate the existence, location, and availability of originals where the unit of description consists of copies.

Rule: Use when the original is NOT held by the State Library of NSW.

Apply according to the principles, and follow the general guidelines in:

• ISAD (G) 3.5.1 Existence and location of originals

With reference to:

• DACS 6.1 Existence and Location of Originals

When describing Graphic Materials refer to:

• DCRM (G) 7B13. Characteristics of original of a copy

When describing Moving Image materials (Pilot project 2020) refer to:

• RDA 27.1 Related Manifestation
