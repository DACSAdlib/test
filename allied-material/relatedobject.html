Adlib Archive Catalogue – Allied Materials – Related object

Requirement: Use only on significant collections

Use this field to show relationships between significant archival materials that are 'related by provenance, sphere of activity, or subject matter' when:

• The SLNSW holds the related material

• The related materials are part of discrete descriptive units and do not form part of the same record hierarchy

• Where the relationship described is significant and this cannot be adequately expressed using creator, author/artist access points or subject headings

• Where the relationship is logical, with regard to the record level and complexity of the related descriptive units.

Before using the field refer to:

• ISAD (G) 3.5.3 Related units of description

In addition to the general rules and exceptions within:

• DACS 6.1 Existence and Location of Originals

DO NOT APPLY

• Advice in ISAD and DACS to refer to related archival materials held in external archives.

Addition of notes:

Where the nature of the relationship between related materials is not made apparent by a title or statement of responsibility:

• Within the Related Object - Notes field, use concise introductory wording and explain the nature of the relationship.
