Adlib Archive Catalogue – Allied Materials – Existence and location of copies

Requirement: As appropriate

Used to indicate the existence, location and availability of copies of the unit being described, that are to issued instead of originals

This element may be used at all levels of description.

Refer to dropdown menu options.

Apply according to the principles, and follow the general guidelines in:

• ISAD (G) 3.5.2 Existence and location of copies

With reference to:

• DACS 6.2 Existence and Location of Copies

• Do not refer to copies held in other institutions, unless there has been an agreement to use these as access copies - Refer to DQSS for advice

When describing Graphic Materials refer to:

• DCRM (G) 7B 22 Additional physical format

When describing Moving Image materials (Pilot Project 2020) refer to:

• RDA 27.1 Related Manifestation
